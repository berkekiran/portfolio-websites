About

Hello! I’m Berke Kiran a self-taught 3D Character Artist and Front-End Developer. I am specialized in creating 3D characters for cinematics, feature film, and games. I love art, science, coding, learning new technologies & improving myself. I was born and grew in Turkey. So I can speak Turkish, English and little bit German. I started to learning game creation at 13 years old. Now I am living to learn and create something new every day. I graduated high school in 2015 with a class rank of second Out of 724 graduates. Then I honored with Outstanding Achievement Certificate & now going to KB.University in Karabuk. I'm learning computer engineering.
